## Market Context

## Entry Logic

- In a mature trading range, buy low and sell high with limit-order logic rather than breakout-chasing.
- Only research this as a framework until range maturity and transaction-cost assumptions are frozen.

## Stop Logic

- Protect beyond the range edge or abandon if the range converts to a trend.

## Target Logic

- Scalp toward the range midpoint or opposite edge; costs and slippage are first-order constraints.

## Invalidation

- Breakout converts into trend with follow-through.

## No-Trade Conditions

- Immature range, news shock, or insufficient spread/cost margin.

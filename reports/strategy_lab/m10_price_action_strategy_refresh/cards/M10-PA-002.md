## Entry Logic

- Enter with the breakout only when the breakout bar is strong and at least one follow-through bar supports it.
- A small-start breakout is acceptable if subsequent bars create a tight channel or higher-timeframe trend bar.

## Stop Logic

- Use the breakout leg extreme or a volatility-adjusted stop; reduce size when stop distance is large.

## Target Logic

- Use measured move from the breakout range and partial exits at prior support/resistance magnets.

## Invalidation

- Follow-through bar reverses strongly or closes back inside the breakout range.

## No-Trade Conditions

- Weak breakout in a trading range with no second bar confirmation.

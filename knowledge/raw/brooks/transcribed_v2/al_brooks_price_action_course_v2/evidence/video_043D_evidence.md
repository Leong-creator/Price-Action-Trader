- `advanced p478`: `assets/evidence/video_043D/advanced_p0478.webp`，同一多头腿入场使用同一止损，新强 BO 后追踪止损。
- `advanced p479`: `assets/evidence/video_043D/advanced_p0479.webp`，合理止损均可，`100+ trades` 后利润接近。
- `advanced p480`: `assets/evidence/video_043D/advanced_p0480.webp`，最低目标 `1x Actual Risk`；强趋势可在 `1x Initial Risk` 首次止盈，风险以相应低点下方 `1 tick` 定义。

- `advanced p486`: `assets/evidence/video_043D/advanced_p0486.webp`，HL 和 TR 下方大空头 BO 后剩余多头放弃，市场 AIS。
- `advanced p487`: `assets/evidence/video_043D/advanced_p0487.webp`，跌破趋势线形成更平坦通道，最终转 TR。
- `advanced p488`: `assets/evidence/video_043D/advanced_p0488.webp`，一旦变 TR，停止像趋势交易；上涨概率不再 `60%`，而是 `50%/50%`。
- `advanced p489`: `assets/evidence/video_043D/advanced_p0489.webp`，所有通道终会结束，`25%` 强 BO，`75%` 变平变宽并转 TR。
